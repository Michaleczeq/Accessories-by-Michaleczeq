[green]Accessories by Michaleczeq[normal]
Modification adds to game accessories for trucks.

Models made by Michaleczeq using SCS Blender Tools.


[white]##############################################################[normal]

Accessories list:
[orange]
* CMR
* Xmas Tree
* CB Radio
* Rubik's cube
* Snowman
* Xmas Bubbles (8 variants)
* Cups (3 variants and 8 looks)
* Wolf Head (LED)
